SELF ENABLEMENT

Start by choosing a knowledge track, and go over collecting badges from each training. IBM has numerous recourses for training, and additionally you are also welcomed to take badges from any other known knowledge sources, such as edX, MITx, Corsera, etc.

Our aim is to guide you in attaining the most important skills to Innovate.
WARM UP

Once you are confident enough in your skills, it is time to get tested. IBM has created some challenges to validate your skills by developing an application in Bluemix. You will have access to an online portal and will be able to select a challenge to work with. At the same portal you will schedule a date to present to an IBM examiner board.

Opportunity for you to prove yourself. You can take your time to create the application.
QUALIFY

It is Pitch Time, you will have 15 minutes to make a bold pitch about your application created during the warm-up phase. After the pitch, you will have 25 minutes to present your company and how your business works. In the end, IBM will take 20 minutes to explore both your technical skills and other business aspects.

RESIDENCE

If the IBM board approves your company, you will be invited to stay for 1-2 weeks working together with our developers in our co-working environment (you will have 2 seats available for developers). This is the last mile to become an official BMX Top Cop and begin taking advantage of our partnership.
